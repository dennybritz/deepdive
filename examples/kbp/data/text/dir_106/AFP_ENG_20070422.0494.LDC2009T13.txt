

French international number eight Thomas Lievremont has signed a
two-year deal with Dax, candidates for a return to top flight
French rugby union next season.


The 33-year-old, who has 37 caps for France, has spent the past
seven seasons with neighbouring Biarritz.


At Dax, he joins his older brother Marc, a former France
international winger, who is a coach with the south western
French outfit.
