

Right-wing candidate Nicolas Sarkozy leads Socialist Segolene
Royal by 54 percent to 46 in voting intentions for the May 6
second round of France's presidential election, an opinion poll
showed Sunday.


Conducted by telephone on a sample of more than 1,000 just after
the two candidates were declared winners in the first round, the
IPSOS poll found that 88 percent of those questioned are sure of
their vote for May 6.
