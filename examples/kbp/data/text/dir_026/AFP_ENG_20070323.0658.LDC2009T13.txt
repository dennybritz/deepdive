

Russian rescue services were continuing Saturday a search for
survivors after finding the wreckage of a helicopter with the
remains of only two of the six people on board, RIA Novosti news
agency reported.


The Mi-8 helicopter, belonging to Gazpromavia, a subsidiary of
the Gazprom energy group, stopped responding to radio
communications at around 1200 GMT Wednesday while flying over the
northern Komi region.


There were five crew and a passenger on board.
