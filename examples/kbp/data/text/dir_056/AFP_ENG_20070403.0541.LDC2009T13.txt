

DaimlerChrysler AG saw US sales fall four percent in March to
228,047 vehicles after weakness at the struggling Chrysler Group
offset gains at the luxury Mercedes division.


Quarterly group sales fell three percent to 593,234 vehicles.


Chrysler division sales fell five percent to 206,435 vehicles in
March and were down four percent for the first quarter of 2007 at
537,249 vehicles.


Sales at Mercedes were up one percent in March to a record 21,612
vehicles and climbed nine percent for the quarter at 55,985
vehicles.
