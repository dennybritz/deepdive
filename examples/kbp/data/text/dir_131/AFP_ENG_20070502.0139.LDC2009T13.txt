

Dutch Princess Ariane, the nearly one-month old daughter of Crown
Prince Willem- Alexander, was admitted to a hospital with a
respiratory infection, the ANP news agency reported Wednesday.


The new born princess is currently undergoing tests and is not in
the intensive care unit, the royal information service told ANP.


Princess Ariane, granddaughter of Dutch Queen Beatrix is the 4th
in line for the throne behind her father and her two sisters. She
was born April 10 in The Hague.
