

Rennes on Wednesday signed Sweden international Petter Hansson
from Dutch side Heerenveen, the French first division club
announced.


"We've reached agreement with Petter Hansson and he's just signed
a three-year contract," Rennes manager Pierre Dreossi said.


The 29-year-old fullback has 21 international caps to his name.


Rennes are flying high in the French first division in sixth and
vying for a UEFA Cup spot next season.
