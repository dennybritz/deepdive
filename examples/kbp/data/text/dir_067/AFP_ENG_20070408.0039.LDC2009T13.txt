

Two ships collided off China's east coast early on Sunday leaving
20 crew of a Cambodian vessel missing, Chinese state media
reported.


The Cambodian ship sank immediately after colliding with a
Chinese cargo vessel at around 4:00 am local time, Xinhua news
agency said.


The agency was quoting marine police in Taizhou city in eastern
China's Zhejiang province.


No other details about the accident or rescue efforts were
immediately available.
