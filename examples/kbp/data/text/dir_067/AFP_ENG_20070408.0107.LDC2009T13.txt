

Israeli troops shot dead a Palestinian after he stabbed two
soldiers outside the Tomb of the Patriarchs in the flashpoint
West Bank town of Hebron, wounding one seriously, security and
medical sources said.


"Two soldiers were injured by a Palestinian in Hebron. He was
shot dead," an army spokesman told AFP.


According to the Magen David rescue service, one of the soldiers
was taken to a Jerusalem hospital with serious wounds after he
was stabbed in the back. The other sustained light wounds.
