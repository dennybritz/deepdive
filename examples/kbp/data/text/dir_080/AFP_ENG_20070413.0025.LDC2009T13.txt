

Hong Kong share prices finished the Friday morning session 0.25
percent lower, reversing modest early gains as property stocks
succumbed to interest rate worries, dealers said.


They said many investors moved to the sidelines ahead of key
inflation data in the US and a meeting of G7 finance ministers
and central bankers this weekend.


The Hang Seng Index ended the morning down 50.50 points at
20,329.71, off a low of 20,264.99 and a high of 20,432.01.
