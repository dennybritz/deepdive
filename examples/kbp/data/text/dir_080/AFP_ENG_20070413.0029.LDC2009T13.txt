

South Korea's top striker Park Ji-Sung of English Premier League
team Manchester United has signed a long-term contract with
sportswear company Nike, the firm's local affiliate said Friday.


"Nike has renewed its contract with Park," said a spokeswoman for
Nike Sports Korea, adding the new contract runs until May 2019,
as compared to the previous four-year one.


"Park merits our sponsorship in light of his achievement and
influence in the world of football," she said.


Nike did not reveal the value of the deal but sports dailies here
said it was believed to be worth more than 12 million dollars.
