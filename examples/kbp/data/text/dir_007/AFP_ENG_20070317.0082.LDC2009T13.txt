

Germany's Magdalena Neuner notched up her seventh women's World
Cup biathlon victory of the season in the 10km pursuit here on
Saturday to set up an intriguing finale.


Germany's Kati Wilhelm, who finished 20th, leads the overall
standings with the final event of the season on Sunday but
Sweden's Anna-Carin Olofsson and Andrea Henkel of Germany, who
finished second and third respectively on Saturday, also have a
chance of winning overall.
