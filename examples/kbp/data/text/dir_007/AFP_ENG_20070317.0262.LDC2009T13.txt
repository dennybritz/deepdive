

Ireland skipper Trent Johnston won the toss and elected to field
against Pakistan in the World Cup Group D match at Sabina Park
here on Saturday.


The Irish, celebrating St. Patrick's Day, are playing an
unchanged 11, but Pakistan have brought Mohammad Sami and Azhar
Mahmood into their 11 for Rana Naved-ul-Hasan and Danish Kaneria


Pakistan lost the opener against West Indies by 54 runs on
Tuesday, but Ireland tied their opening match with Zimbabwe on
Thursday. West Indies lead the group with two points, Ireland and
Zimbabwe have one point apiece, and Pakistan have no points.


Ireland:


Jeremy Bray, William Porterfield, Eoin Morgan, Kevin O'Brien
(wkt), Andre Botha, Niall O'Brien, Kyle McCallan, Trent Johnson
(capt), Andrew White, Dave Langford- Smith, Boyd Rankin.


Pakistan: Mohammad Hafeez, Imran Nazir, Younis Khan, Mohammad
Yousuf, Inzamam- ul-Haq (capt), Shoaib Malik, Kamran Akmal (wkt),
Azhar Mahmood, Mohammad Sami, Rao Iftikhar, Umar Gul.


Umpires: Billy Bowden (NZL) and Brian Jerling (RSA); TV Replays:
Simon Taufel (AUS), Reserve: Ian Gould (ENG). Match referee:
Chris Broad (ENG).
