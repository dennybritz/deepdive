

India captain Rahul Dravid elected to bat after winning the toss
against Bangladesh in the World Cup Group-B match at the Queen's
Park Oval here on Saturday.


Both the teams are playing their first match, while Sri Lanka
posted a 243-run victory over debutants Bermuda in their opening
game here on Thursday.


The top two sides will make it to the next Super Eight stage.


Both India and Bangladesh had a light workout in bright sunshine
before the toss.


India: Rahul Dravid (capt), Sachin Tendulkar, Sourav Ganguly,
Robin Uthappa, Yuvraj Singh, Virender Sehwag, Mahendra Dhoni,
Ajit Agarkar, Harbhajan Singh, Zaheer Khan, Munaf Patel.


Bangladesh: Habibul Bashar (capt), Shahriar Nafees, Tamim Iqbal,
Aftab Ahmed, Saqibul Hasan, Mohammad Ashraful, Mushfiqur Rahim,
Mohammad Rafique, Abdur Razzak, Mashrafe Mortaza, Syed Rasel.


Umpires: Aleem Dar (PAK) and Steve Davis (AUS)


TV umpire: Ian Howell (RSA)


Match referee: Alan Hurst (AUS)
