

The new daughter of Dutch Crown Prince Willem-Alexander and his
Argentinian-born wife Princess Maxima has been named Ariane
Wilhelmina Maxima Ines.


The prince announced the names Friday when he officially reported
the birth at the The Hague old city hall.


Princess Ariane, who was born Tuesday, is fourth in line to the
Dutch throne after her father, and her sisters Princesses
Catharina-Amalia and Alexia.
