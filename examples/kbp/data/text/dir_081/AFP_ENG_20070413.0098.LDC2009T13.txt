

Chinese share prices closed 0.36 percent lower Friday as
investors booked profits after nine consecutive days of record
gains, dealers said.


The benchmark Shanghai Composite Index, which covers A and
B-shares, closed down 12.76 points at 3,518.27 on turnover of
166.49 billion yuan (21.54 billion US dollars).


The Shanghai A-share Index ended down 13.77 points or 0.37
percent at 3,698.45 on turnover of 165.57 billion yuan while the
Shenzhen A-share Index finished down 0.32 points or 0.03 percent
at 992.94 on turnover of 90.32 billion yuan.
