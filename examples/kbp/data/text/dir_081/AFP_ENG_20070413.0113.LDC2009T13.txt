

The euro rose to 1.3534 dollars on Friday in early European
trading, its highest level since January 3, 2005.


At 0730 GMT, the euro was trading at 1.3529 dollars from 1.3480
dollars late on Thursday.


The European single currency broke through 1.35 dollars on
Thursday after the European Central Bank signalled that it was
ready to raise eurozone borrowing costs again in June.
