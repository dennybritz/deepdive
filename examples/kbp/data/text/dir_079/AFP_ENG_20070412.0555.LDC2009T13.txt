

The Taliban on Thursday said they had "hit" a NATO helicopter in
southwest Afghanistan, while an ISAF spokesman said "there had
been an incident" without giving any details.


"We hit a NATO helicopter in Ghazni province," Youssouf Ahmadi, a
Taliban spokesman told AFP by telephone.


Angela Billings, a spokeswoman for the International Security
Assistance Force (ISAF) told AFP that "there was an incident in
the eastern command region," of which Ghazni is a part, but
declined to give any more details.


The Ghazni authorities could not immediately be contacted.
