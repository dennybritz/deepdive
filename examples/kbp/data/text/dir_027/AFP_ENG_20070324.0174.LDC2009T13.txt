

Kenyan Absel Kiprop won the world junior men's cross country
title here on Saturday.


Kiprop led home a Kenyan 1-2-3-4 as he crossed the line ahead of
Vincent Chepkok and Mathew Kisirio while Leonard Komon was
fourth.


It was the second successive cleansweep for the host nation as
earlier Linet Barasa won the junior women's title, leading home
Mercy Kosgei and Veronica Wanjiru.
