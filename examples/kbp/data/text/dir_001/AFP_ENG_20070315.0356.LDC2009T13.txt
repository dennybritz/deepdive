

The following is line-up of the new 25-member Palestinian
national unity government unveiled on Thursday:


Prime Minister: Ismail Haniya (Hamas)


Deputy Prime Minister: Azzam al-Ahmed (Fatah)


Foreign Minister: Ziad Abu Amr (independent)


Finance Minister: Salam Fayyad (independent - member of Third Way
bloc)


Interior Minister: Hani al-Qawasmeh (independent)


Education Minister: Nassereddin al-Shaer (Hamas)


Information Minister: Mustafa al-Barghuti (independent)


Justice Minister: Ali al-Sartawi (Hamas)


Culture Minister: Bassam al-Salhi (Palestinian People's Party)


Health Minister: Radwan al-Akhras (Fatah)


Transport Minister: Saadi al-Krunz (Fatah)


Labour Minister: Mahmud al-Alul (Fatah)


Social Affairs Minister: Saleh Zeidan (Democratic Front for the
Liberation of Palestine)


Prisoner Affairs Minister: Taysir Abu Sneineh (Fatah)


Planning Minister: Samir Abu Eisheh (Hamas)


Local Government Minister: Mohammed al-Barghuti (Hamas)


Economy Minister: Ziad al-Zhazha (Hamas)


Youth and Sports Minister: Bassem Naim (Hamas)


Communications and Information Technology Minister: Yusef al-
Mansi (Hamas)


Waqf (Islamic endowment) Minister: Ismail al-Shandi (Hamas)


Agriculture Minister: Mohammed al-Agha (Hamas)


Women's Affairs Minister: Mariam Saleh (Hamas)


Tourism Minister: Khulud Dwaibess (independent)


Public Works Minister: Samih Shbeib (Fatah)


Minister of State: Wasfi Qabha (Hamas)
