

Los Angeles is in the grip of the driest year since record-
keeping began in the 1870s, official figures showed Tuesday.


Despite recent rain in April, so far only 3.21 inches of rain has
fallen on the city, the National Weather Service revealed in its
latest figures.


Average rainfall in Los Angeles is nearly 15 inches per year.


The previous record low rain season in Los Angeles was in
2001-2002, when 4.42 inches of rainfall was recorded.
