

Slovakia's Daniela Hantuchova easily beat second seeded Svetlana
Kuznetsova 6-3, 6-4 in the women's final of the ATP/WTA Pacific
Life Open on Saturday.


Hantuchova captured her second career singles title with both of
championships coming at the tier one WTA event at the Indian
Wells Tennis Garden stadium.


Hantuchova had 27 winners and 14 unforced errors to Kuznetsova's
22 winners and 19 unforced errors in the one hour, 23 minute
match in front of a crowd of 11,500.


Kunzetsova still has a 5-3 career lead against Hantuchova since
their first meeting in 2003 in Leipzig, Germany.
