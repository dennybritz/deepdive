

Iran on Monday pardoned two Swedish nationals jailed for
photographing military installations last year and will release
them later in the day, the foreign ministry spokesman said.


"Within the framework of the humanitairian efforts of Iran and
following the requests of the family and repeated requests from
Swedish politicians... they have been pardoned and will be freed
at 6:00 pm" (1530 GMT), foreign ministry spokesman Mohammad Ali
Hosseini said in a statement.
