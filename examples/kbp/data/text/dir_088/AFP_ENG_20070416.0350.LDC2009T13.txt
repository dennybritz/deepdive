

The parents of a Colombian governor have been kidnapped in
neighboring Venezuela, police in Bogota said Monday.


Authorities said the parents of Governor Jose Luis Gonzalez
Crespo were abducted from their farm in the town of El Guarero,
in the neighboring Venezuelan state of Zulia.


"The parents of the governor of La Guajira have been kidnapped,"
said Carlos Usunaga, chief of police for the northern Colombia
department.


"We are coordinating with Venezuelan authorities," he told RCN
radio, adding that there have been no ransom demands so far.
