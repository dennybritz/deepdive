

Hong Kong's busy international airport has set across-the-board
air traffic records as regional economic expansion saw an
increase in air travel, it said Sunday.


The airport handled 45 million passengers during the financial
year ended April 2007, up 8.5 percent on the previous year as
aircraft movements rose 4.8 percent to 283,000.


Its cargo record also tumbled with the throughput of 3.57 million
tonnes of freight, up 2.8 percent.
