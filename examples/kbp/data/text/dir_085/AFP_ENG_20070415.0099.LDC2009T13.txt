

Shami Mubarak of Qatar won the Paris Marathon on Sunday in a time
of 2 hours 07 minutes 17 seconds with Ethiopia's Gashaw Melese in
second and Daniel Rono of Kenya third.


The women's race was won by Tafa Magarsa of Ethiopia in 2:25:06
ahead of Russia's Gulnara Vigovsakaya and Christelle Daunay of
France.
