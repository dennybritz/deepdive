

Denmark's Crown Princess Mary gave birth Saturday to a baby girl,
her second child and third in line to the throne after Crown
Prince Frederik and the couple's son Prince Christian, the palace
said.


"On Saturday, April 21, 2007 at 4:02 pm, Her Royal Highness Crown
Princess Mary gave birth to a healthy daughter at Rigshospitalet
(Copenhagen University Hospital) in Copenhagen," the palace said
in a statement.


"Mother and baby are doing fine," it said, adding that Crown
Prince Frederik was present at the birth.


Australian-born Mary Donaldson, 35, married Prince Frederik, 38,
in May 2004. Frederik is the eldest son of Queen Margrethe II.


Prince Christian was born on October 15, 2005.
