

Israeli Prime Minister Ehud Olmert said on Sunday that he was
ready attend a summit with Arab leaders to discuss a Saudi-
drafted peace plan.


"I am announcing to the heads of the Arab states on this
occassion that if the Saudi king initiates a meeting of moderate
Arab states and invites me and the head of the Palestinian
Authority in order to present us the Saudi ideas, we will come to
hear them and we will be glad to voice ours," Olmert told a joint
press conference with German Chancellor Angela Merkel in
Jerusalem.
