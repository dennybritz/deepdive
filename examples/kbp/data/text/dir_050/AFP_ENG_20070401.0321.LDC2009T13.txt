

Theo Bos of the Netherlands won the men's coveted sprint crown on
the final day of the world track cycling championships here
Sunday.


Bos, the defending champion, dominated Gregory Bauge over two
legs in the final to leave the young Frenchman with a world
championship best silver medal.


Another Frenchman, Mickael Bourgain, took the bronze after
outclassing Britain's Craig MacLean in their two leg match.
