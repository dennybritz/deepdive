

Katherine Bates of Australia won the women's points race crown on
the final day of the world track cycling championships here
Sunday.


Mie Bekker Lacota of Denmark took the silver medal while New
Zealand's Catherine Cheatley finished third to take the bronze.


It is Australia's second gold medal of the competition after Anna
Meares' world record-breaking ride to the gold in the 500 metre
time trial.


Bates finished top of the table with 35 points gained from the 10
intermediate sprints in the 25km race, six ahead of her Danish
rival.


Cheatley finished third on 27.
