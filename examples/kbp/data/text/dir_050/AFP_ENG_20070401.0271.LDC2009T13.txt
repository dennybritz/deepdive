

Franco Marvulli and Bruno Risi combined to win the men's Madison
gold medal for Switzerland on the final day of the world track
cycling championships here Sunday.


Switzerland snatched the gold medal in the last of the 50km,
200-lap race's 10 intermediate sprints, claiming a precious two
points to finish on a total of 14 and pip Dutch pair Peter Schep
and Danny Stam to the title.


The Netherlands finished second on 13 points to take the silver
medal while Czech duo Alois Kankovsky, a gold medal winner in the
men's inaugural omnium, and Petr Lazar finished on 11 to win the
bronze.


Reigning champions Spain finished fourth on eight points.
