

At least 32 workers were killed Wednesday and two injured when
they were showered by molten steel at a metal factory in
northeast China, state press said.


The accident happened in a workshop of the Qinghe Special Steel
Corporation in Tieling city in Liaoning province, a center of
heavy industry, Xinhua news agenct reported.


It was triggered when a steel ladle used for pouring molten steel
sheared off from the blast furnace and fell onto workers below,
spilling molten metal, according to Xinhua.
