

Russian oil, gas and coal production rose slightly in the first
quarter on a 12-month basis while electricity output fell back,
the energy and natural resources ministry said Thursday.


Production of oil and condensed gas rose 4.2 percent in the first
quarter to 121.14 million tonnes compared with the same period
last year, with Lukoil, Rosneft, TNK-BP, Surgutneftgas and
Gazprom Neft the top producers.


Gas output edged up 0.2 percent to 174.74 billion cubic meters,
with Gazprom accounting for 147.99 billion cubic meters.


Production of coal gained 1.9 percent to 81.28 million tonnes,
while electricity output slipped 1.6 percent to 275.29 billion
kilowatts an hour.
