

Italian cyclist Stefano Garzelli won the 176km third stage of the
Tour of Trentino between Cavareno and Toscolano Maderno in
northern Italy on Thursday.


It was the 2000 Tour of Italy champion's first win since August
of last year.


Ukrainian Mikhaylo Khalilov was second, while Damiano Cunego came
in third to retain the overall lead.
