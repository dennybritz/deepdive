

Tasmania claimed their first-ever Australian Sheffield Shield
cricket title with a thumping 421-run victory over New South
Wales early on the last day of the five-day final at Bellerive
Oval here Friday.


The Tasmanians, hosting the final for the first time since
joining the competition in 1977, dismissed NSW for 149 to claim
victory just after lunch.


NSW were chasing 571 runs to win but succumbed to the pace
bowling of Damien Wright (5-13) and Ben Hilfenhaus (4-22).


Match scores: Tasmania 460 and 340, NSW 230 and 149.
