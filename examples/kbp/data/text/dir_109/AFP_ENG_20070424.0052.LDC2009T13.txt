

New Zealand share prices closed 0.93 percent lower Tuesday, led
down by a sharp fall in market leader Telecom, dealers said.


The benchmark NZX-50 gross index fell 38.53 points to 4,163.11 on
light turnover worth 83.6 million dollars (62.0 million US).
Falls outnumbered rises 79 to 36 among the 155 stocks traded.
