

German rider Markus Fothen (GST) won the 157.8 km first stage of
the Tour de Romandie on Wednesday, from Granges-Paccot to La
Chaux-de-Fonds.


Italian Paolo Savoldelli (AST) retained the leader's yellow
jersey.


Spaniard Francisco Perez (GCE) was second in a sprint finish,
followed by Caisse d'Epargne teammmate Joaquin Rodriguez.
