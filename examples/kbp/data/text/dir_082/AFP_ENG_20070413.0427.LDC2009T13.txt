

This year's Turkish Super Cup is set to be held in Germany again
the German Football Federation confirmed on Friday.


The previous edition was held in Frankfurt, Germany, between
Besiktas and Galatasaray and drew a crowd of 30,000.


The date and the venue for the match - which brings together the
domestic champions and Cup winners - has yet to be decided.


Germany is home to millions of Turkish immigrants and people of
Turkish descent.
