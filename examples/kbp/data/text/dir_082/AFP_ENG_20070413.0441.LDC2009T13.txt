

Australia were 77-1 after 10 overs of their World Cup Super
Eights match against Ireland here on Friday chasing just 92 to
win.


Michael Hussey was on 18 with Andrew Symonds on 15.


Adam Gilchrist was the first man dismissed for 34 when he was
clean-bowled by Ireland's Australian-born captain Trent Johnston
in the ninth over with the score on 62.


Earlier, Glenn McGrath took 3-17 and Shaun Tait 3-39 to put
Australia in sight of the semi-finals.


At one stage, Ireland were 12-4 on a pitch having pace, bounce
and movement.
