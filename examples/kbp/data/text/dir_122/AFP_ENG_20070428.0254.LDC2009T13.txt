

Rain reduced the World Cup final between Australia and Sri Lanka
here at Kensington Oval on Saturday to a 38 overs per side
contest.


Three bowlers will be permitted to bowl a maximum of eight overs
each and two a maximum of seven.


The match is due to start at 12.15pm local time (1615GMT) nearly
three hours after the scheduled start.


Double-defending champions Australia have won the toss and
elected to bat.
