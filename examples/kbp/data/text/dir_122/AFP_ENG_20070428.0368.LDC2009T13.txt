

An accident during a classic car race on Saturday in Austria
caused 14 spectators to be injured, emergency workers said.


Two people were seriously hurt during the "Classic Rally of
Southern Styria" near Gamlitz, some 170 kilometres (100 miles)
south of Vienna.


Police said one of the cars involved in the race collided with
another car parked near the course, pushing it toward a crowd of
spectators. Police were investigating to determine precise
details of the crash.


Cars dated before 1950 participated in the race.
