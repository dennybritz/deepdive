

German police have arrested a 48-year-old German man suspected of
being behind 19 murders between 1974 and 2000 in Germany, France
and Spain, the European justice coordination agency Eurojust said
here on Monday.


Eurojust said "Thanks to successful investigations by the Spanish
authorities, an alleged serial killer has been arrested,"
Eurojust said.


He is a 48-year-old German lorry driver who traveled regularly
from Germany to mainly France and Spain, it said.
