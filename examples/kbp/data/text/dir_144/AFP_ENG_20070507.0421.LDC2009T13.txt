

Oscar-winning actor-director Kevin Costner has become a father
for the fifth time after the birth of his first child with wife
of two years, Christine Baumgartner, it was reported on Monday.


Costner, 52, and Baumgartner, 33, have named their baby boy
Cayden Wyatt Costner following his birth on Sunday, celebrity
news websites reported.


Costner has three children from an earlier marriage to childhood
sweetheart Cindy Silva and a fourth from a relationship with
Bridget Rooney.


The movie star married Baumgartner, a German handbag designer, in
2004.
