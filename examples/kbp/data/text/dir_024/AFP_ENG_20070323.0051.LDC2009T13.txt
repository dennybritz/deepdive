

UN Secretary General Ban Ki-moon has arrived in Egypt on the
second leg of a Middle East tour aimed at re-energising the peace
process, the official MENA agency said Friday.


Ban arrived late Thursday night from Baghdad. He is due to meet
Egyptian President Hosni Mubark, Foreign Minister Ahmed Abul
Gheit and Arab League Secretary General Amr Mussa during his two-
day trip.


He will also be visiting Israel, the Palestinian territories,
Jordan, Saudi Arabia and Lebanon on his regional tour.
