

Brazilian forward Salles has signed a contract to play for
J-League first division club Omiya Ardija from the Atlanta
Silverbacks, club officials said Wednesday.


His contract runs from March 27 to January 1 next year, the
officials said.


The 29-year-old Brazilian, whose full name is Mauricio Salles de
Alencar, could play after he was registered by the Japan Football
Association, the officials said.


Omiya, who finished 12th last season, have had three straight
defeats and are currently second from bottom in the 18-team
division this season.
