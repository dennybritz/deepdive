

At least eight policemen were among more than a dozen people
killed in a suicide attack targetting a police headquarters in
eastern Afghanistan, police and a doctor said.


"We have got so far eight policemen dead, whose bodies were
brought to our hospital," doctor Gul Mohammaddin Mohammadi from
the Khost city hospital told AFP.


"Six other cops were also brought with injuries," he said.


Police said some civilians were also killed in the attack.


Khost police commander Qasem Khil said more than a dozen people
including policmen and civilians were killed and injured.
