

Three people were killed in an armed attack on a publishing house
in the eastern Turkish city of Malatya, media reports said.


The Anatolia news agency said the circumstances of the attack
were not immediately clear and that one person who jumped from
the building during the attack survived with injuries.


The NTV news channel said the publishing house, Zirve, printed
books on Christianity, among others. Two of the dead were
employees, it said.


The Malatya police chief and a prosecutor were investigating at
the scene, Anatolia said.
