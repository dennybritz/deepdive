

Eight villagers in southern India died after being struck by
lightning during a Hindu ceremony, police said Wednesday.


The Tamil Nadu state government said the villagers were in a shed
filling copper pots with holy water for the ceremony late Tuesday
when the lightning struck.


Eight of the worshippers died on the spot while five others,
including an 11 -year-old boy, were hospitalised.


The government statement said each of the bereaved families would
receive compensation of 100,000 rupees (2,350 dollars).
