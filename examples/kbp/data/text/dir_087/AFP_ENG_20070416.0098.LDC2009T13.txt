

At least 22 people were killed and 20 others injured when a
passenger bus and an oil tanker collided in northern Tanzania,
police and medics said Monday.


The accident occurred after the two vehicles smashed into each
other in the Kilimanjaro region late Sunday, a police official
told AFP.


"Investigations on the cause of the accident are still going on,"
he added.


The injured were rushed to the nearby Kilimanjaro Christian
Medical Centre.
