

Hong Kong share prices opened 0.75 percent higher Monday,
tracking gains on Wall Street and in Tokyo after tame US
wholesale inflation data, dealers said.


The US Labor Department announced on Friday that the producer
price index rose 1.0 percent in March, smaller than than the 1.3
percent gain in February.


The Hang Seng Index opened up 152.29 points at 20,493.26.
