

The International Basketball Federation (FIBA) has suspended
Latvian Kaspars Kambala for 14 months after he tested positive
for drugs.


The 28-year-old "has been suspended by FIBA for a period of 14
months, after testing positive for metabolites of cocaine, in
compliance with FIBA's internal regulations governing doping
control and the WADA Anti-Doping Code".


Kambala, who played this season's Euroleague with Turkish club
Fenerbahce, will not play again before February 12, 2008.
