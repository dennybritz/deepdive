

Result of an Olympic Games Africa zone second round, second leg
qualifying match at the weekend:


Ghana 2 (Sadat Boukari 55, 64) Burkina Faso 0


Ghana win 5-0 on aggregate


Note: Burkina Faso fielded two ineligible players when winning
the first leg 2-0 in Ouagadougou last month and world football
governing body FIFA awarded Ghana a 3-0 victory
