

European stock markets were mixed in initial trading on Thursday,
with London's FTSE 100 index of leading shares adding just 0.02
percent to 6,366.10 points.


Frankfurt's DAX 30 index gained a marginal 0.01 percent to
7,074.59, while in Paris the CAC 40 fell 0.10 percent in value to
5,733.20 points.
