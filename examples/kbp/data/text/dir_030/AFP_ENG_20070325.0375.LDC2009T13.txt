

A plane traveling from Frankfurt to Cairo made an emergency
landing in Belgrade airport on Sunday after the pilots reported a
fire in the cockpit, Serbian state television reported.


The Lufthansa Airbus 300/600 plane carrying 213 passangers
requested permission to land in the Serbian capital around 5:40
pm (1540 GMT), the television said.


No passengers, as well as 11 crew members, were hurt, while
firemen had taken out an electronic device producing sparks, the
report said.


Nebojsa Nedeljkovic, Belgrade's airport general manager told the
television the plane was to continue its flight later Sunday.
