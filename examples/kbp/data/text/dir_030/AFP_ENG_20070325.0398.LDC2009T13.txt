

Second-half goals by Jhon Viafara and Andres Chitivas lifted
Colombia past Switzerland 3-1 in an international football
friendly here Sunday.


Edixon Perea opened the scoring for the South Americans in the
21st minute but Swiss striker Alexandre Frel equalized on a
penalty kick in the 38th minute.


Viafara put Colombia ahead to stay in the 55th minute while
Chitivas added an insurance tally six minutes before full time.


The match, played before 10,000 spectators at the Orange Bowl,
served as a Colombian tuneup for June's Copa America.
