

A Russian helicopter with six people on board has disappeared in
the far north of Russia, the Interfax news agency reported on
Wednesday.


The Mi-8 helicopter, belonging to Gazpromavia, a subsidiary of
the Gazprom energy group, stopped responding to radio
communications at around 1200 GMT while flying over the northern
Komi region.


There were five crew and a passenger on board.


Search efforts continued until nightfall without any sight of the
helicopter and are due to begin first thing Thursday.
