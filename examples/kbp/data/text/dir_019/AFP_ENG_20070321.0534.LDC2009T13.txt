

The charred body of a Nigerian man, known to police in connection
with drug crimes, was discovered near the southern Italian city
of Naples, police said in a statement on Wednesday.


Idehen Bright's body, which was discovered Tuesday evening on a
street in the neighbourhood of Giugliano near Naples, was
identified through partially burnt documents on his person,
according to the statement.


A preliminary investigation showed that Bright had died after
being struck on the head with a blunt object.
