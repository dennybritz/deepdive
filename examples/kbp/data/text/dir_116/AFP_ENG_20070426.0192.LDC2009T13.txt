

Dutch chemicals and pharmaceutical group Akzo Nobel announced on
Thursday it will invest 250 million euros (341 million dollars)
in the company's new facilities to be built in Ningbo, China.


"The two new facilities - for the manufacture of ethylene amines
and chelating agents- wil be the first to be constructed on what
will be one of the biggest sites for the company's activities in
the world," Akzo said in a statement.


Akzo Nobel already operates eight chemical production plants in
China, while its coatings business has 13 facilities.
