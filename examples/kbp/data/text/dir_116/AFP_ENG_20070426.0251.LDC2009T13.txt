

India's biggest private company, Reliance Industries, Thursday
reported fourth quarter net profit rose a better than expected 14
percent, led by higher sales at its core oil refining business.


For the three months ended March 31, net profit rose to 28.53
billion rupees (699 million dollars), beating market forecasts of
27.3 billion rupees, on sales which rose 5.5 percent to 259.8
billion rupees.


Full-year net profit jumped 20.1 percent to 109 billion rupees.
