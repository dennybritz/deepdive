

Top Spanish clubs Barcelona and Sevilla were drawn apart in the
Spanish Cup semi-final draw on Thursday.


Barcelona, the reigning Champions League winners, host Getafe for
the first leg of this April 9/18 tie, while Deportivo entertain
reigning UEFA Cup champions Sevilla in the first match.


Spanish Cup semi-finals


Deportivo La Coruna v Sevilla


Barcelona v Getafe


Ties: April 9/18
