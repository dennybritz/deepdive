

Ryder Cup organisers on Wednesday announced dates of September
27-October 3 for the 2010 edition which is to be held at the
Celtic Manor Resort near Newport in south Wales.


Europe retained the trophy in Ireland in September last year and
the Americans will play host at Valhalla, Kentucky in 2008.


Former Masters and British Open champion Nick Faldo will captain
the European side in Wales in 2010.
