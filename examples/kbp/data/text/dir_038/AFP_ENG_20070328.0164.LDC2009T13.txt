

Turin Olympic champion Yevgeny Plyushchenko said Wednesday he was
set to return and continue his figure skating career.


"Russian figure skaters put in poor performances at the latest
world championships in Japan," Saint Petersburg's city council
deputy Plyushchenko said during a break in the assembly's
schedule.


"I would like to return and to win more medals for our country.
And I would like to combine my sports performances with my work
as a deputy here."


His coach Alexei Mishin confirmed that he and Plyushchenko were
planning to resume practice sessions in the summer adding that
they were also planning to take part at the 2010 Winter Games in
Vancouver.
