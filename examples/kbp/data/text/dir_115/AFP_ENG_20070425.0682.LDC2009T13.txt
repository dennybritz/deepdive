

A relatively strong earthquake measuring 5.4 on the Richter scale
hit southwestern Japan on Thursday, the Japan Meteorological
Agency said.


The quake, which happened just after 9:00 am (0000 GMT), was in
Ehime prefecture on Shikoku island, one of Japan's four main
Japanese islands, it said.


The quake was 40 kilometres deep. There was no immediate report
of damage.
