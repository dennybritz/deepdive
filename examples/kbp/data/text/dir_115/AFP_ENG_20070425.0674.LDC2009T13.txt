

Uruguayan President Tabare Vazquez will head to the United Arab
Emirates and Qatar on Saturday to strengthen trade and diplomatic
ties with the Gulf countries, government sources said Wednesday.


Vazquez will be accompanied by a delegation that includes
business leaders and five cabinet members, including the foreign,
economy and tourism ministers, sources from his office said.


Vazquez will leave for Abu Dhabi on Saturday before going to
Dubai on Tuesday and Doha on Wednesday.


"The trip aims to strengthen political relations, expand trade
and gain investments for infrastructure for the country's main
tourist destinations," a presidential source said.
