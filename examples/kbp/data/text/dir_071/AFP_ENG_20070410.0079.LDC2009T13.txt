

Europe's main stock markets climbed in initial trading on Tuesday
as traders returned to their desks after the Easter break.


London's FTSE 100 index of leading shares gained 0.19 percent to
6,409.40 points, Frankfurt's DAX 30 index won 0.03 percent to
7,102.47 points and in Paris the CAC 40 advanced 0.16 percent to
5,750.47.


European markets were shut on Friday and Monday for Easter public
holidays.
