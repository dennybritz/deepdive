

Australian share prices closed up 1.25 percent at a record high
Tuesday with investors buoyed by merger and acquisition activity,
dealers said.


The benchmark SP/ASX 200 closed up 75.9 points at a record
6,153.0, beating last Wednesday's previous record of 6,097.2.


The broader All Ordinaries index rose 72.1 points to a record
6,136.1, also surpassing Wednesday's record level of 6,079.0.
