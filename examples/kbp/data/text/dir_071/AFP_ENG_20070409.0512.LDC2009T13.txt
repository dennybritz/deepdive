

Miami Heat swingman James Posey was arrested on drink driving
charges early Monday, American newspapers reported.


The Palm Beach Post said that Posey, 30, was arrested in Miami
Beach and taken to Miami-Dade County Jail, where he was held on
1,000 dollar bond.


According to police reports, Posey's car was stopped in the
middle of a two-lane road and and he was talking to pedestrians
and occupants of other cars before being stopped by police.


Posey missed four games earlier this season after being suspended
for failing to meet the team's conditioning requirements. He is
averaging 7.9 points and 5.1 rebounds in 67 games.
