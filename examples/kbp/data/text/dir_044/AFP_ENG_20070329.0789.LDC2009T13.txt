

Australian Open champion Serena Williams reached the final of the
6.9 million- dollar WTA and ATP Masters Series tournament here
Thursday with a straight-set win over Israel's Shahar Peer.


Williams, seeded 13th, defeated 14th-seeded Peer 7-6 (7/4), 6-1
to book a clash with second seed and newly installed world No. 1
Justine Henin of Belgium.


Henin advanced earlier Thursday with a 6-2, 6-3 win over Russian
Anna Chakvetadze.
