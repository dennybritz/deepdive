

Belgian police, hunting a missing 18-year-old woman since the
weekend, found a bag containing an unidentified dead body in a
canal on Thursday, Belga news agency said.


The bag was fished out of the canal around 12 kilometres (7
miles) from where the woman, Annick Van Uytsel, went missing
after leaving a party on Friday evening.


It was sent to a nearby hospital for examination, Belga said.
Investigating prosecutors were not immediately available for
comment.
