

Australian rider Robbie McEwen (PRL) won the 166.9 km second
stage of the Tour de Romandie on Thursday, from the Swiss town of
La Chaux-de-Fonds to Lucens.


Italian Paolo Savoldelli (AST) retained the leader's yellow
jersey.


Slovenian Borut Bozic (LPR) was second, followed by Italian
Enrico Gasparotto (LIQ).
