

A strong 6.2-magnitude earthquake hit Japan's southwestern
islands on Friday but there were no immediate reports of damage
or casualties, officials said.


The quake occurred at 9:26 am (0026 GMT) northwest of Miyako
Island in the East China Sea with its epicentre located 20
kilometers (12 miles) below the seabed, the meteorological agency
said.


There was no danger of a tsunami, it said.
