

World 100m champion Lauryn Williams of the United States and
Olympic 200m champion Veronica Campbell of Jamaica will meet in a
100m feature here at a Grand Prix meet, organizers announced
Thursday.


The sprint showdown on June 2 will send Williams, who also won
world gold on the 4x100m relay and a 2004 Olympic silver medal,
against Campbell, who won Athens Olympics gold in the 200m and
4x100m relay as well as 100m bronze.


Tyson Gay and training partner Wallace Spearmon, a pair of US
sprint stars, lead the men's lineup. Gay wil run the 100 while
world championship 200m runner-up and US 200m champion Spearmon
will race at 200m.
