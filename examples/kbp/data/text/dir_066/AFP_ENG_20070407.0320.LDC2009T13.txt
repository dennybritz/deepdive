

France's Tatiana Golovin defeated sixth seed Ana Ivanovic of
Serbia 6-4, 3-6, 6-4 in the semi-finals of the 600,000 dollar WTA
Amelia Island tournament on Saturay.


Golovin, the world's 20th-ranked player who was seeded eight,
advances to Sunday's final where she will face the winner of the
other semi-final between top seeded Russian Nadia Petrova and No.
13 Sybille Bammer of Austria.
