

Some 40 people have been killed and more than 70 injured in
fierce sectarian clashes between Sunni and Shiite Muslims in
northern Pakistan, officials said Saturday.


"So far we have received reports that 40 people have been killed
in clashes in two days of fighting between two religious groups
in Parachinar," said Arif Habib, a top official dealing with
security in tribal areas bordering Afghanistan.
