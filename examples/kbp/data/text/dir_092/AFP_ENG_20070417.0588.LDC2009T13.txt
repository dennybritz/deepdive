

South Africa beat England by nine wickets at the Kensington Oval
here on Tuesday in their World Cup Super Eights match and book
their place in the semi-finals.


Brief scores


England 154 all out in 48 overs (A Strauss 46; Hall 5-18)


South Africa 157-1 in 19.2 overs (G Smith 89 not out, AB de
Villiers 42)
