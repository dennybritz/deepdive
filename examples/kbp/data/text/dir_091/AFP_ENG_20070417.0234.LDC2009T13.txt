

Iran is working continuously and using all its efforts to install
uranium- enriching centrifuges at its key nuclear plant in
Natanz, the head of its atomic energy agency told the ISNA news
agency on Tuesday.


"We are employing the maximum effort to realise our projects in
Natanz. Today in Natanz there is a continuous movement to install
the centrifuges," Gholam Reza Aghazadeh told the agency.
