

Twelve people died in western Ukraine Tuesday after the minibus
in which they were travelling was crushed by a lorry, said
tranport police.


The accident, in the Khmelnitski region of Ukraine, happened
after the driver of a Renault heavy goods vehicle fell asleep at
the wheel and crashed into the Mercedes minibus.


"The massive impact completely crushed the minibus, whose driver
and 11 passengers died from their injuries at the scene," said
the police statement.


The driver of the lorry was injured and had been admitted to
hospital, it added. The police are in the process of identifying
the victims.
