

Fifteen British sailors released by Iran after two weeks in
detention left Tehran on Thursday on a flight back home to
Britain, Iran's state-run television reported.


"The 15 British sailors left the the Iranian capital for London a
few minutes ago on board a British Airways flight," Iran's Arabic
language channel Al-Alam said.


"The plane has left," added the newsreader on Iran's rolling news
channel IRINN.
