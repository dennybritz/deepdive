

Australian share prices jumped 1.5 percent to a fresh record
close Wednesday as Wall Street's positive lead lifted the market,
dealers said.


The benchmark SP/ASX 200 closed up 91.9 points at 6,237.7,
beating the previous record set on April 18.


The All Ordinaries also topped its previous best, adding 84.3
points to close at 6,224.5.
