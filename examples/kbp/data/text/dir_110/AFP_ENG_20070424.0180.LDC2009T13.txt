

English Premiership side Reading have signed Cameroon
international Andre Bikey on a three-year deal from Lokomotiv
Moscow.


The 22-year-old defender has been on loan with Reading since the
start of the season and has slowly established himself as a
regular in Steve Coppell's side.


The transfer fee is believed to be around 1 million pounds
(1.5million euros) and the move is subject is to ratification by
the Premier League and the Football Associations of both England
and Russia.
