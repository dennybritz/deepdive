

French presidential rivals Nicolas Sarkozy and Segolene Royal
will stage a televised debate on Wednesday May 2, Sarkozy's
campaign director Claude Gueant said Tuesday.


The debate will go out at 9.00 p.m. (1900 GMT) on the TF1 and
France 2 television channels, he said after a meeting between
representatives of the candidates at the broadcast watchdog the
Higher Audiovisual Council.


The debate will be screened four days before the decisive second
round of the presidential election.
