

China recorded 19 million new cellphone users, equivalent to the
entire population of Scandinavia, in the first three months of
the year, the government said Friday.


At the end of March, the number of Chinese mobile phone users hit
480.7 million, up from 461.1 million at the end of last year, the
Ministry of Information Industry said.


Fixed-line users increased to 371 million from 367.8 million
during the period, the ministry said in a statement on its
website.


The telecommmunications industry earned revenue of 165.1 billion
yuan (21.4 billion dollars) in the first three months, up 8.4
percent year-on-year, according to the ministry.
