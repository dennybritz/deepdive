

Europe's main stock exchanges steadied in initial trade on
Friday, despite another record run overnight on Wall Street, with
London's FTSE 100 index up 0.24 percent at 6,553.70 points.


In Paris the CAC 40 index added a marginal 0.01 percent to
6,004.62, while Frankfurt's DAX 30 nudged down by 0.04 percent to
7,476.68 points.
