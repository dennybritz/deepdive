

Thirteen people were killed when a minibus and a truck ran into
each other in Russia's southern Voronezh region, Russian news
agencies reported early Tuesday.


The Gazel van and the Volvo truck collided head-on, the RIA
Novosti reported quoting local emergency situations ministry
officials, adding that three people, including the truck's
driver, were rushed to a hospital.


According to an ITAR-TASS report, all 13 killed were Uzbek
nationals.
