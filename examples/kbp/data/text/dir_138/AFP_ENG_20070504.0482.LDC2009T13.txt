

Police from nine European countries arrested 35 suspected drug
traffickers during a joint operation involving 13 international
airports, Portuguese police said Friday.


Besides Portugal, participating countries were Germany, Belgium,
Spain, France, Italy, Ireland, Luxembourg and Britain. The
operation occurred over the course of four days last month,
Portuguese police said in a statement.


In addition to 125 kilos (275 pounds) of cocaine, police also
seized 64 kilos (141 pounds) of hashish and 7.3 kilos of heroin
arriving from South America and the west coast of Africa.
