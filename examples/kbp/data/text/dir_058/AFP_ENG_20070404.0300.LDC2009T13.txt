

Iranian President Mahmoud Ahmadinejad on Wednesday handed out a
medal of bravery to the commander of Iranian forces who arrested
15 British sailors on accusations of entering its waters.


"Here I want to thank with a medal of third degree bravery to the
commander of the forces who defended Iran's borders and arrested
the intruders," Ahmadinejad told a news conference.


He then presented the medal to Revolutionary Guards Naval
Commander Aboul Ghassem Ammanghah.
