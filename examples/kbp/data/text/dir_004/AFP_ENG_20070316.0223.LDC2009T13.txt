

EU foreign policy chief Javier Solana and German Foreign Minister
Frank-Walter Steinmeier will visit Washington next week to
prepare a EU-US summit in April, officials said on Friday.


Solana, Steinmeier and the EU's External Relations Commissioner
Benita Ferrero- Waldner will meet with US Secretary of State
Condoleezza Rice on Monday, a German foreign ministry spokesman
said.


Germany currently holds the rotating six-month EU presidency.


The EU-US summit will take place in Washington on April 30.


The spokesman said Steinmeier would also discuss issues including
Iran's nuclear programme, the situation in the Middle East and
the future status of Kosovo.
