

Laos on Friday reported its second confirmed human death from
bird flu, a woman from Vientiane province who died on March 4, in
a statement issued jointly with the World Health Organisation.


"Results from a World Health Organisation Reference Laboratory in
Tokyo have confirmed the second human case of avian influenza
H5N1 in the Lao People's Democratic Republic," the health
ministry said in a statement.


Laos on March 8 announced a 15-year-old girl had been confirmed
as the country's first human death from the virus.
