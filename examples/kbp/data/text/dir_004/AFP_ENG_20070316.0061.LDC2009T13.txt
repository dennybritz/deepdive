

At least 12 people died and 49 were injured after a bus they were
travelling in collided head on with a lorry in central Tanzania,
police said on Friday.


The accident occurred near the town of Morogoro, some 255
kilometres (160 miles) west of Dar es Salaam, on Thursday
evening, said regional police commander Samuel Mpasa.


The bus had been travelling to the capital, Dodoma.


"Those injured were rushed to Morogoro hospital for treatment,"
Mpasa said, adding that police were investigating the cause of
the accident.
