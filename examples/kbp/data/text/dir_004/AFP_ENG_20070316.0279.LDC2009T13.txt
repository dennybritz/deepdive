

Wet conditions due to overnight and early morning rain delayed
the toss in the World Cup Group A match between South Africa and
the Netherlands at Warner Park here on Friday.


Groundsmen were busy trying to dry the outfield and the two
umpires Mark Benson of England and Tony Hill of New Zealand were
scheduled to make an inspection of the pitch and outfield at
09:30 am (1330 GMT).


More showers were forecast later in the day.


Defending champions Australia beat Scotland by 202 runs in the
opening match of the group on Wednesday.
