

Honda Motor's joint venture in China is recalling 528,406
passenger cars and minivans due to quality defects, a spokeswoman
for the Japanese automaker said Friday.


The recall by Guangzhou Honda Automobile Co Ltd affects 488,606
Odyssey minivans with steering or other defects and 39,800
Accord, Odyssey and Fit vehicles with a glitch with the fuel pump
relay system, she said.


She said the recall was not expected to spread outside of China.


"At this point, I don't know of any plan to announce a recall
elsewhere," the spokeswoman added.
