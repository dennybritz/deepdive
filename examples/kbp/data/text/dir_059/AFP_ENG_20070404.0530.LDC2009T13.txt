

The 15 British sailors released by Iran will be handed over to
the British embassy on Thursday, the top aide to President
Mahmoud Ahmadinejad said, the Mehr news agency reported.


"The 15 released British sailors will be handed over to the
British embassy tomorrow and head for London," Mojtaba Samareh
Hashemi said. "The British sailors are at the moment at the
foreign ministry."
