

Following is the list of ministers in the new Estonian coalition
government, led by Prime Minister Andrus Ansip.


The government is made up of the right-of-centre Reform Party,
the conservative Pro Patria-Res Publica and the left-wing Social
Democratic Party.


Prime Minister Andrus Ansip (Reform)


Foreign Minister Urmas Paet (Reform)


Justice Minister Rein Lang (Reform)


Culture Minister Laine Janes (Reform)


Social Affairs Minister Maret Maripuu (Reform)


Environment Minister Jaanus Tamkivi (Reform)


Agriculture Minister Helir-Valdor Seeder (Pro Patria-Res Publica)


Minister for Education and Science Tonis Lukas (Pro Patria-Res
Publica)


Minister for Economic Affairs and Communication Juhan Parts (Pro
Patria-Res Publica)


Defence Minister Jaak Aaviksoo (Pro Patria-Res Publica)


Minister for Regional Development Vallo Reimaa (Pro Patria-Res
Publica)


Finance Minister Ivari Padar (Social Democrats)


Interior Minister Juri Pihl (Social Democrats)


Minister for Population Urve Palo (Social Democrats)
