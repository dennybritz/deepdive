

President Mahmoud Ahmadinejad on Wednesday met the 15 British
sailors as they were released at the presidential compound,
wishing them success for the future, state televison pictures
showed.


"We are are grateful for your forgiveness," one of the sailors
was heard telling the president. All the men were wearing blue or
grey suits. The sole woman captive, Faye Turney, has not yet been
shown.
