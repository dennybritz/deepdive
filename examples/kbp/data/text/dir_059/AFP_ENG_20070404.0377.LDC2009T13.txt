

Barcelona will face Egyptian giants Al-Ahly in a friendly in
Cairo on April 24, the defending Spanish champions said Wednesday
on their Internet site.


The deal to stage the match, part of Al-Ahly's centenary
celebrations, was signed Tuesday in the Egyptian capital by the
presidents of both clubs, it said.


Cairo-based Al-Ahly are the most crowned club in Egypt, having
won the Egyptian League 31 times. It currently leads the Egyptian
league.


Barcelona met in Cairo before in a friendly in 1961 with the
Spanish side scoring a 6-1 win.
