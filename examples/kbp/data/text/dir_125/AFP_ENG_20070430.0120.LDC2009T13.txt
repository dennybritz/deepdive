

Barcelona Open winner Rafael Nadal remained in second place in
the ATP rankings behind Swiss rival Roger Federer on Monday,
however the Spaniard's recent win gives him command of this
season's ATP Race.


Argentine Guillermo Canas, beaten by Nadal in Sunday's final,
moved up six places to 22nd in the rankings while Frenchman Paul-
Henri Mathieu moved up 15 places to 42nd thanks to his victory in
Casablanca.
