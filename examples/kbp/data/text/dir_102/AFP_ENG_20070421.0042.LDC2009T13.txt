

Nigerian police said had they foiled an attempt on Saturday to
burn down the electoral commission's offices in the federal
capital Abuja using a tanker lorry full of fuel and a gas
cylinder.


"There was an attempt to burn down the electoral commission's
office this morning around 4 am (0300 GMT). A fuel tanker fully
loaded with petrol was seen moving towards the office and there
was no one inside", police spokesman Haz Iwendi told AFP.
