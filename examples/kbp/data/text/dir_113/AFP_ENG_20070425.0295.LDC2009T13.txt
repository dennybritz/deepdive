

Two Italian journalists arrested in the Nigerian federal capital
Abuja have been released, ANSA news agency quoted the Italian
foreign ministry as saying on Wednesday.


Emanuele Piano and Marco Ricchello, freelance journalists for the
leftist daily Liberazione, were arrested earlier that day. Piano,
35, informed the newspaper by SMS text message, ANSA said.


It quoted Liberazione's editor-in-chief, Claudi Iampaglia, as
saying the reporters had been covering Saturday's presidential
elections in Nigeria and were to travel to the volatile southern
Niger Delta region.
