

A host of current and former world leaders was expected at the
funeral of Russia's first post-Soviet president Boris Yeltsin on
Wednesday. Among them were:


- former US president Bill Clinton


- former US president George H.W. Bush


- last Soviet leader Mikhail Gorbachev


- former British prime minister John Major


- Britain's Prince Andrew


- Polish former president and Solidarity leader Lech Walesa


- German President Horst Koehler


- EU External Relations Commissioner Benita Ferrero-Waldner


- French Foreign Minister Philippe Douste-Blazy


- former Canadian prime minister Jean Chretien


- Estonian President Toomas Hendrik Ilves


- Lithuanian President Valdas Adamkus


- Kazakh President Nursultan Nazarbayev


- Belarussian President Alexander Lukashenko


- Armenian President Robert Kocharian


- Ukrainian Prime Minister Viktor Yanukovych


- former Ukrainian president Leonid Kuchma


- former Kyrgyz president Askar Akayev


- former Finnish president Mauno Koivisto


- former Italian prime minister Giulio Andreotti


- former South Korean prime minister Han Myeong-Sook


- Georgian parliament Speaker Nino Burdzhanadze
