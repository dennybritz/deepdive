

New Zealand captain Stephen Fleming elected to bat after winning
the toss against debutants Ireland in a World Cup Super Eights
match at the Providence Stadium here on Monday.


New Zealand are unbeaten in the tournament with six points from
three matches, while Ireland have yet to open their account after
three games.


New Zealand: Stephen Fleming (capt), Shane Bond, James Franklin,
Peter Fulton, Brendon McCullum, Craig McMillan, Jacob Oram,
Jeetan Patel, Scott Styris, Daniel Vettori, Hamish Marshall.


Ireland: Trent Johnston (capt), Kyle McCallan, Jeremy Bray, Peter
Gillespie, Dave Langford-Smith, Eoin Morgan, Kevin O'Brien, Niall
O'Brien, William Porterfield, Boyd Rankin, Andrew White.


Umpires: Steve Bucknor (WIS) and Simon Taufel (AUS)


TV umpire: Billy Doctrove (WIS)


Match referee: Ranjan Madugalle (SRI)
