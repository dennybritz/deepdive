

Australian Open champion Serena Williams booked a semi-final
berth at the 6.9 million-dollar Sony Ericsson Open here Wednesday
with a straight-set win over Nicole Vaidisova.


Williams, the 13th seed, defeated the eighth-seeded Czech 6-1,
6-4 to set up a clash with Israeli Shahar Peer, a 6-0, 6-3 winner
earlier in the day over Italy's Tathiana Garbin.


In the other semi-final, world number one Justine Henin of
Belgium will take on Russian Anna Chakvetadze.
