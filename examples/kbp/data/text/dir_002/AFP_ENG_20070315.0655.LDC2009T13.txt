

New Zealand scrum-half Byron Kelleher has agreed to join French
Top 14 side Agen for two seasons at the end of the World Cup
later this year.


The club said Thursday that the 30-year-old, who has been capped
49 times by the All Blacks, had confirmed by telephone that he
will join for the two years.


Kelleher currently plays for the Waikato Chiefs in the southern
hemisphere Super-14 competiton.
