

Motorcycling star Casey Stoner powered his MotoGP Ducati to
victory in the Grand Prix of China here Sunday in a tightly
fought contest against seven-time world champion Valentino Rossi.


The Australian completed the Shanghai race in 44mins 12.891secs,
with Italy's Rossi on a Yamaha 3.036 seconds off the pace and
American John Hopkins on a Suzuki finishing third for his first-
ever podium.
