

India on Friday selected separate Test and one-day squads for
next month's tour of Bangladesh:


Test squad: Rahul Dravid (capt), Wasim Jaffar, Sachin Tendulkar,
Venkatsai Laxman, Sourav Ganguly, Yuvraj Singh, Mahendra Singh
Dhoni, Dinesh Karthick, Anil Kumble, Ramesh Powar, Rajesh Pawar,
Shanthakumaran Sreesanth, Zaheer Khan, Vikram Rajvir Singh, Munaf
Patel.


One-day squad: Rahul Dravid (capt), Gautam Gambhir, Robin
Uthappa, Virender Sehwag, Yuvraj Singh, Manoj Tewari, Dinesh
Mongia, Mahendra Singh Dhoni, Dinesh Karthick, Shanthakumaran
Sreesanth, Munaf Patel, Zaheer Khan, Rudra Pratap Singh, Piyush
Chawla, Romesh Powar.


Tour cricket manager: Ravi Shastri
