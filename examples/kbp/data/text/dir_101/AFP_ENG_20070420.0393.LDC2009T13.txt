

European stock exchanges powered ahead Friday, ending two days of
losses, as the London FTSE 100 index gained 0.72 percent to end
the week at 6,486.80.


In Paris the CAC 40 rose 1.88 percent to 5,938.90, its highest
reading since January 2001, while in Frankfurt the Dax added 1.38
percent to reach 7,342.54.
