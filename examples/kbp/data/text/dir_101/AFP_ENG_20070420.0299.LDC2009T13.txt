

A Bulgarian plane with 72 passengers on board made an emergency
landing on Friday at Athens International Airport due to a
technical problem, an airport press officer told AFP.


The plane operated by Hemus Air was on a flight from Sofia to
Athens when a problem occurred in one of its two engines, state
television NET reported.


The airport was on a "total alert" footing but the landing was
successful without further incident, the airport officer said.
