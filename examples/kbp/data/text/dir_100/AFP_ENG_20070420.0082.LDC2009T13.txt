

Europe's main stock markets rose in opening deals on Friday
following two days of losses.


London's FTSE 100 index of leading shares climbed 0.20 percent to
6,453.40 points, Frankfurt's DAX 30 advanced also 0.20 percent to
7,257.34 points and in Paris the CAC 40 index gained 0.47 percent
to 5,856.50.
