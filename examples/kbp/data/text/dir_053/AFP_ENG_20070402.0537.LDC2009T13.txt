

New Zealand were 47-1 after 10 overs of their World Cup Super
Eights match against Bangladesh at the Sir Vivian Richards
Stadium here Monday needing 175 to win.


Captain Stephen Fleming was on 28 with Hamish Marshall on one.


Peter Fulton was the first man out caught at mid-on by Tamim
Iqbal off Syed Rasel for 15 in the 10th over.


Bangladesh had been dismissed for 174 in their innings with Scott
Styris taking four wickets and Jacob Oram picking up three.
