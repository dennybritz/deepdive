

New Zealand captain Stephen Fleming won the toss and elected to
field against Bangladesh in the World Cup Super Eights match at
the Sir Vivian Richards Stadium here Monday.


Bangladesh made two changes from the side that sufered a
10-wicket thrashing by world champions Australia here Saturday.


Opening batsman Javed Omar replaced the out of form Shahriar
Nafees and left-arm seamer Syed Rasal came in for Tapash Baisya.


New Zealand were unchanged from the team that beat hosts the West
Indies by seven wickets here Thursday.
