

United States airline Continental said that from Monday it had
increased the frequency of its non-stop Hong Kong-New York
service from six a week to seven.


The boosted service, which is now the most frequent of any
American airline on the route, links Hong Kong with Newark
Liberty Airport in New Jersey via Boeing 777-200 aircraft.


Continental is among the largest airlines in the world, with
3,100 daily departures serving 136 international and 150 US
destinations.
