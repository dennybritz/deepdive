

New Zealand international back Sam Tuitupou on Monday signed a
deal until 2010 with English Premiership rugby strugglers
Worcester.


Tuitupou, who currently plays with Waikato Chiefs, captained New
Zealand's under-19 team to the 2001 world title and the under-21
side to the 2004 crown.


The 25-year-old won the first of his nine caps for the All Blacks
on June 12, 2004 against England.


Worcester are fighting relegation and are are sitting just one
point above Northampton at the bottom of the Premiership table.
