

Belgian Justine Henin reclaimed the world number one ranking from
Russian Maria Sharapova on Monday without even playing  last
week.


Henin benefited from Sharapova's shock fourth round exit during
her title defence in Indian Wells.


Slovak Daniela Hantuchova moves up six places to 12th after
winning her second career singles title and second in Indian
Wells Saturday at the expense of Russian Svetlana Kuznetsova.


Kuznetsova achieves a career-high ranking as she takes the number
three spot from France's Amelie Mauresmo, who missed the
California tournament.
