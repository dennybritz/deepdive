for the Euro2008 qualifier against Luxembourg on March 24:


Goalkeepers: Vasily Khomutovsky (Tomsk/RUS), Yury Zhevnov (FC
Moscow/RUS), Vladimir Gayev (Odessa/UKR)


Defenders: Sergei Omelianchuk (Rostov/RUS), Sergei Shtanyuk
(Vladivostok/RUS), Dmitry Lentsevich (Torpedo Moscow/RUS),
Alexander Yurevich (Soligorsk), Artem Chelyadinsky
(Zaporozhie/UKR), Artem Radkov (BATE Borisov)


Midfielders: Timofei Kalachev (Rostov/RUS), Vladimir Korytko
(Odessa/UKR), Alexander Kulchy (Tomsk), Oleg Stakhanovich
(Kaunas/LIT), Dmitry Likhtarovich (BATE Borisov), Denis Kovba
(Samara/RUS), Alexander Hleb (Arsenal/ENG)


Forwards: Sergei Kornilenko (Dnepropetrovsk/UKR), Artem Kontsevoi
(MTZ-RIPO Minsk), Vyacheslav Hleb (Kaunas/LIT), Vitaly Kutuzov
(Parma/ITA), Leonid Kovel (Lviv), Gennady Bliznyuk (BATE Borisov)
