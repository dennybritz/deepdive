

European stock markets fell in opening deals on Monday -- the
first trading day of the second quarter -- with London's FTSE 100
index of leading shares dropping 0.23 percent to 6,309.60 points.


Frankfurt's DAX 30 index dipped 0.09 percent to 6,911.13 points
and in Paris the CAC 40 lost 0.22 percent to 5,621.50.
