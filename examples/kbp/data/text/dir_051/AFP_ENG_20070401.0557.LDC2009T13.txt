

Hong Kong share prices opened the first trading session of the
second quarter 0.5 percent higher Monday as property stocks and
select blue chips saw fresh buying interest, dealers said.


The gains ar expected to be capped ahead of upcoming holidays,
with the market to be closed from Thursday until next Monday for
the Ching Ming Festival and the Easter holidays.


The benchmark Hang Seng Index opened up 99.08 points at
19,900.01.
