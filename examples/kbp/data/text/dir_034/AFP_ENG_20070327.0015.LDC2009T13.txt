

Seven Vietnamese sailors have been rescued from a freighter that
began sinking after colliding with another ship off southern
China, state media reported on Tuesday.


The sailors were rescued by Chinese maritime authorities off the
coast of the Guangxi region on Monday before their ship went
under, Xinhua news agency said, adding they were in stable
conditions.


The ship was carrying 900 tonnes of coal when the accident
occurred. The report provided no information on the other vessel.
