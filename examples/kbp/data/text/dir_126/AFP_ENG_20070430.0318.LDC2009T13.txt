

The struggling China Team sprang a surprise over leaders BMW
Oracle to record its first win in 12 America's Cup qualifying
races here on Monday.


China Team, who are bottom of the standings with no previous
wins, beat the American boat, in first place with a sole defeat,
by more than three minutes.


The BMW Oracle boat suffered problems with its large fore sail at
the start of the race and China Team swept past for a comfortable
win.
