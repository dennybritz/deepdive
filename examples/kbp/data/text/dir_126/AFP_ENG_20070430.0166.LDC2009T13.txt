

Annual inflation in Italy slowed to 1.5 percent in April from 1.7
percent in March, the national statistics institute Istat
reported Monday.


Prices rose 0.2 percent in April from March, when they also
increased 0.2 percent when compared with February.


According to a European Union harmonized index, Italian inflation
in April came to 0.5 percent over one month and 1.7 percent when
compared with April last year.
