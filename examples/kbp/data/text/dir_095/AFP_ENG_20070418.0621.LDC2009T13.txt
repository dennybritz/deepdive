

An Italian tourist was killed and 13 others were injured in a bus
accident Wednesday near the central city of Khenifra, a news
agency reported.


The bus overturned while heading toward Marrakech, Map news
agency said. An investigation had been opened to determine the
cause.


Three Italians were seriously injured in the crash and were taken
to a hospital in Meknes, east of Rabat. Three Moroccans had minor
injuries.
