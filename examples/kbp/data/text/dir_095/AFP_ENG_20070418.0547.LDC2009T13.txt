

Spains's Prime Minister Jose Luis Rodriguez Zapatero will visit
Russia later this year, Foreign Minister Miguel Angel Moratinos
said Wednesday.


"Zapatero will visit Moscow this year," he told a joint news
conference with visiting Russian counterpart Sergei Lavrov,
adding that the trip would take place "in the coming months"


"It won't happen any later than this autumn," he said.


This year marks the 30th anniversary of the restoration of
diplomatic relations between the two countries.
