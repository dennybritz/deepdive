

Bangladesh captain Habibul Bashar elected to field after winning
the toss against Sri Lanka in a World Cup match at the Queen's
Park Oval here on Wednesday.


Both the teams have won their opening matches in Group B, with
Sri Lanka defeating debutants Bermuda by 243 runs and Bangladesh
beating former champions India by five wickets.


India defeated Bermuda by a record 257 runs for their only win in
two matches. The top two sides will advance to the next Super
Eight stage.


Sri Lanka and Bangladesh retained the sides which won the first
matches.


Sri Lanka: Mahela Jayawardene (capt), Kumar Sangakkara, Sanath
Jayasuriya, Upul Tharanga, Tillakaratne Dilshan, Russel Arnold,
Chamara Silva, Chaminda Vaas, Farveez Maharoof, Lasith Malinga,
Muttiah Muralitharan.


Bangladesh: Habibul Bashar (capt), Shahriar Nafees, Tamim Iqbal,
Aftab Ahmed, Saqibul Hasan, Mohammad Ashraful, Mushfiqur Rahim,
Mohammad Rafique, Abdur Razzak, Mashrafe Mortaza, Syed Rasel.


Umpires: Daryl Harper (AUS) and Steve Davis (AUS)


TV umpire: Ian Howell (RSA)


Match referee: Jeff Crowe (NZL)
