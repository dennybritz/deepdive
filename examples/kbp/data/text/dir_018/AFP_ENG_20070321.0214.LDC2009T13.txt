

A new award billed as the "Oscars of Sports" aiming to honour
Asia's top sporting stars was launched Wednesday by officials in
Malaysia.


The six award categories are: Asian sportsman of the year; Asian
sportswoman of the year; Asian team of the year (men); Asian team
of the year (women); a Lifetime achievement award; and a
Humanitarian award.


Thirty-one Asian Sports Press Union (ASPU) affiliates, the
Olympic Council  of Asia and Olympic councils in 45 countries
will be requested to submit nominations for the Asian Sports
Awards, officials said.
