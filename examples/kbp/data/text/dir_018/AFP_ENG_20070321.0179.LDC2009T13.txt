

French President Jacques Chirac Wednesday gave his backing to
right-wing presidential candidate Nicolas Sarkozy, announcing
that the interior minister will leave the government on March 26
to focus on the race.


Chirac announced he would give his "vote and support" to the 52
-year-old Sarkozy, the frontrunner for the April-May election, in
a televised statement recorded at the Elysee presidential palace.
