

An Australian snowboarder fell about 100 metres (330 feet) to his
death in the Swiss Alps, police said Sunday.


The 31-year-old man died afer slipping on an icy off-piste
section of slope near Riddes in the southern Swiss canton of
Valais.


Another snowboarder was killed in the same area on Friday, said
the police statement, which did not disclose either victim's
identity.
