

The Netherlands will take on Thailand and South Korea in a
football tournament to be played in Asia in June, the Netherlands
Football Federation (KNVB) said Thursday.


The Europeans will play South Korea, who are coached by Dutchman
Pim Verbeek, in Seoul on June 2 before travelling to Bangkok to
face Thailand four days later.


Both South Korea and Thailand line up in July's Asian Football
Championships, to be played in Thailand, Vietnam, Malaysia and
Indonesia.
