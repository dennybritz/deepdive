

A suicide bomber has exploded his car near a US military patrol
base north of Baghdad killing nine soldiers and wounding 20
others, while a marine died in western Iraq, the military said
Tuesday.


The attack took place in restive Diyala province on Monday, the
military said. One Iraqi civilian was also wounded in the bomb
attack.


Fifteen of the wounded soldiers have returned to duty after
initial medical care, the military added.


Also on Monday, a US marine died "while conducting combat
operations" in western Iraq's Al-Anbar province, according to a
second statment


The latest fatalities took the military's losses in Iraq to 78 in
this month alone and to 3,331 since the March 2003 invasion of
the country, according to an AFP count based on Pentagon figures.
