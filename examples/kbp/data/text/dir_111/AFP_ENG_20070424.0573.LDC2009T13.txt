

Albania's Foreign Minister Besnik Mustafaj has resigned, telling
a press conference here on Tuesday: "I'm feeling tired."


He offered no further explanation for the resignation, which was
presented to Prime Minister Sali Berisha on Monday and must be
accepted to come into effect.


A former journalist and ambassador to France, Mustafaj has been
foreign minister since September 2005. He is a lawmaker for
Albania's Democratic Party.
