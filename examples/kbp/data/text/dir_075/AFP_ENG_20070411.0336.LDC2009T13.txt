

England won the toss and elected to field against Bangladesh in
the Super Eights match at the Kensington Oval here on Wednesday.


England remained unchanged from their seven-wicket defeat against
Australia in Antigua on Sunday, while Bangladesh brought back
vice-captain Shahriar Nafees in place of Javed Omar from their
team which upset South Africa by 67 runs in Georgetown, Guyana
last week.


England: Michael Vaughan (capt), Andrew Strauss, Ian Bell, Kevin
Pietersen, Paul Collingwood, Andrew Flintoff, Paul Nixon, Ravi
Bopara, Sajid Mahmood, James Anderson, Monty Panesar


Bangladesh: Habibul Bashar (capt), Shahriar Nafees, Tamim Iqbal,
Aftab Ahmed, Saqibul Hasan, Mohammad Ashraful, Mushfiqur Rahim,
Mohammad Rafique, Abdur Razzak, Mashrafe Mortaza, Syed Rasel


Umpires: Simon Taufel (AUS) and Steve Bucknor (WIS)


Tv replays: Rudi Koertzen (RSA)


Reserve umpire: Billy Bowden (NZL)


Match referee: Jeff Crowe (NZL)


Pitch: Hard and bouncy, with tinge of grass


Weather: 86 degrees Fahrenheit, maximum humidity 82 percent with
scattered showers.
