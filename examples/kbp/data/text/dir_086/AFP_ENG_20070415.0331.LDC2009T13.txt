

A flight between the Turkish city of Antalya and Dusseldorf in
Germany was forced to make an emergency landing at Istanbul
airport on Sunday due to a damaged instrument panel, media
reported.


The Turkish airline Best Air's flight was carrying 210 passengers
between southern Turkey and eastern Germany when its autopilot
gauge broke down, forcing the plane to land in Istanbul.


Fire fighters and ambulances were waiting for the Airbus 321
plane, the Anatolia news agency reported, adding that no one was
injured in the incident.
