

Carrefour, the world's second-biggest retailer, said on Monday it
had signed an agreement to acquire Atacadao, Brazil's leading
operator of discount hypermarkets, for 825 million euros (1.1
billion dollars).


Carrefour said the transaction would make Carrefour Brazil the
largest food retailer in the country in terms of sales.


Shares in the French company, which is eyeing growth in emerging
economies and especially Latin America. gained 0.51 percent to
57.69 euros on a Paris market which was 0.47 percent lower in
late morning trade.
