

Hong Kong share prices finished the Wednesday morning session
0.17 percent weaker on mild profit-taking following gains over
the last four sessions, dealers said.


They said investors turned cautious ahead of the release of US
Federal Reserve meeting minutes later Wednesday and key US
inflation data in the coming days.


The Hang Seng Index ended the morning down 34.43 points at
20,313.44, off a low of 20,278.84 and a high of 20,438.57.
