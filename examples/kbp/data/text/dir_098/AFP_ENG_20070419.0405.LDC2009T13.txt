

A Swedish JAS Gripen jet fighter crashed on Thursday during an
exercise near the northern town of Luleaa but the pilot was able
to eject safely, the military said in a statement.


The plane was heading in for landing at the Vidsel air base west
of Luleaa when it crashed. The cause of the accident was not yet
known, the military said.


The pilot was able to eject before the crash and was not
seriously injured, it added.
