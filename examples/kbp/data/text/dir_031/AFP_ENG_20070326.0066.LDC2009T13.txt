

Sidi Ould Cheikh Abdallahi, backed by supporters of the country's
ousted long- time dictator, has won the second round of the
presidential election, a source in the Interior Ministry said on
Monday with 99 percent of the votes counted.


Abdallahi has garnered 52.89 percent of the vote, while his rival
Ahmed Ould Daddah has 47.11 percent, according to the source.


The final official results are expected to be announced later on
Monday by the interior minitry.
