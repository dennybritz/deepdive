

China's Lin Yue and Huo Liang won the men's 10m synchronised
platform world title Monday as the Chinese ended the diving
competition here with nine gold out of 10.


The Chinese pair amassed 489.48 points.


Russia's Gleb Galperin and his partner Dmitriy Dobroskok were
second with 467.16 while the United States claimed its first
diving medal here with David Boudia and Thomas Finchum clocking
463.56 points for third.
