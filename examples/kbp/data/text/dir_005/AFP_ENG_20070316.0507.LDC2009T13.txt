

South Africa made 353-3 off 40 overs against the Netherlands in
their World Cup Group A match at Warner Park here on Friday.


Jacques Kallis made 128 not out while Mark Boucher was unbeaten
on 75.


Herschelle Gibbs hit 72 and skipper Graeme Smith, 67.


The match has been reduced to a 40-overs-a-side match because of
rain.
