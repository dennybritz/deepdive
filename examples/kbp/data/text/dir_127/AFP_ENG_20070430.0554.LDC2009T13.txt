

Six people died and several were injured late Monday in a
stampede at a concert held in Sfax, 300 kilometres (200 miles)
south of Tunis, an official said here.


The stampede occurred at a theatre where a concert was being held
for the TV programme "Star Academy Lebanon", the official said.


Details were not immediately available. Rescuers rushed to the
Sidi Mansour theatre, the official added.
