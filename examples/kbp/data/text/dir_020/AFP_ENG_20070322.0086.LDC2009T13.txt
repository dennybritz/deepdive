

European stock markets climbed in opening deals on Thursday, with
London's FTSE 100 index of leading shares adding 0.14 percent to
6,265.70 points.


In Paris, the CAC 40 index rallied strongly, adding 1.43 percent
to 5,580.88 points, while in Frankfurt the DAX 30 increased 0.46
percent to 6,742.78.
