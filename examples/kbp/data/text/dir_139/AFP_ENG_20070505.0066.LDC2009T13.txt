

The governor of the Muslim holy city of Mecca, Prince Abdel Majid
bin Abdul Aziz, died in the United States on Saturday, the
official Saudi Press Agency reported.


Prince Abdel Majid, 65, a half-brother of King Abdullah of Saudi
Arabia, died in hospital in Seattle, Washington, after a long
struggle with illness, a royal source told AFP, declining to
specify the illness.


He is to buried in the capital Riyadh on Sunday.
