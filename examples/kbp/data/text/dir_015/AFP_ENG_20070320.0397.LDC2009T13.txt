

Blackburn's FA Cup semi-final clash with Chelsea has been
scheduled for April 15 at Old Trafford, the Football Association
announced on Tuesday.


The other semi-final, between Watford and Manchester United, will
be at Villa Park on April 14.


FA Cup semi-finals


April 14


Watford v Manchester United, Villa Park (1630 GMT)


April 15


Blackburn v Chelsea, Old Trafford (1500 GMT)
