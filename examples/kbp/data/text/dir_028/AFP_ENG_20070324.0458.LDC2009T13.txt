

Japan issued a tsunami warning Sunday along the west coast of the
main island of Honshu following a strong earthquake, the
meteorological agency said.


The 7.1-magnitude quake occurred in Ishikawa prefecture, about
300 kilometers (200 miles) northwest of Tokyo, at around 9:44 am
(0044 GMT), the agency said.


The tsunami warning was issued for the coast of Noto peninsula
facing the Sea of Japan (East Sea), the agency said.
