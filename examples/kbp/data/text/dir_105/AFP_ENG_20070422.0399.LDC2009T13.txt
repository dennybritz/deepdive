

US forces conducted air and ground strikes against a suspected
meeting of Al- Qaeda militants south of Baghdad on Sunday,
killing 18 of them, the military said.


"Coalition forces conducted air strikes on a known Al-Qaeda
meeting location south of Baghdad that killed 15 terrorists," the
military said.


"Later in the operation, ground forces killed three terrorists
loading a vehicle carrying an anti-aircraft weapon."


Seven other suspected militants believed responsible for
kidnappings or helping to bring in foreign fighters were
detained, the military added.


The raid also uncovered several weapons caches and a second anti-
aircraft gun.


Another individual was detained in a separate raid north of
Baghdad, near Taji.
