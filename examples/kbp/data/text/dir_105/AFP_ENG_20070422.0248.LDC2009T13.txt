

Four people were killed in flooding brought on by three days of
heavy rain in the northeastern Moroccan region of Nador, local
authorities said on Sunday.


Three middle school students, two girls and a boy from the same
family, died in floods in the valley of Amlah in Ain Zohra, west
of Nador city, while a young girl died in flooding in the village
of Zaio, east of Nador, authorities said.
