

Indian police on Wednesday arrested a drunken passenger on a New
Delhi-bound flight from Bangkok after he threatened to blow up
the plane, an airline spokesman told AFP.


State-run airline Indian's flight from the Thai capital was
forced to make an emergency landing in the eastern city of
Kolkata.


"Flight 854 is being searched for explosives," the spokesman,
Ashok Sharma, said.


The detained passenger was only identified as a Mexican citizen,
who was drunk.
