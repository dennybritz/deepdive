

European stock markets faltered in early trading on Wednesday
when the FTSE index of 100 leading shares in London was showing a
fall of 8.6 points or 0.14 percent to 6,211.70 points.


In Frankfurt the Dax index fell by 0.06 percent to 6,696.25
points although in Paris the CAC 40 index edged ahead by 0.13
percent to 5,510.46 points.
