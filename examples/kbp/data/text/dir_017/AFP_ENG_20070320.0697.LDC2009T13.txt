

Gerald Wallace scored 27 points and grabbed 11 rebounds to spark
the Charlotte Bobcats past Cleveland 108-100 in overtime Tuesday,
spoiling a 37-point effort by LeBron James.


Matt Carroll added 20 points while Walter Herrmann added 19
points and 10 rebounds for the Bobcats, who at 25-43 have the
third-worst record in the National Basketball Association.


The Cavaliers sunk to 41-26, falling 2 1/2 games behind Detroit
in the fight for the best record in the Eastern Conference and a
home-court edge into the NBA Finals.


Larry Hughes added 17 points and Lithuania's Zydrunas Ilgauskas
added 15 for the Cavaliers, who led 46-37 at halftime but shot
only 39-of-93 from the field and were outrebounded 46-43.
