

British filmmaker Stephen Frears, who made the award-winning
movie "The Queen", is to head the nine-strong jury that will
award the Palme d'Or at the Cannes film festival next month.


Organisers said Thursday the other eight jury members are:


Maria de Medeiros (Portugal), actress


Maggie Cheung (Hong Kong), actress


Toni Colette (Australia), actress


Sarah Polley (Canada), actress


Marco Bellocchio (Italy), director and writer


Orhan Pamuk (Turkey), novelist, winner of 2006 Nobel Prize for
Literature


Michel Piccoli (France), actor


Abdrahmane Sissako (Mauritania), director
