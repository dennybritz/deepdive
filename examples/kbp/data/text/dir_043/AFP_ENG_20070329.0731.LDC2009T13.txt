

Japan's industrial output fell 0.2 percent in February from the
previous month, beating market expectations which had been for a
0.6 percent decline, official figures showed Friday.


Industrial shipments in February fell 1.4 percent from January
while inventories were down 0.4 percent, according to preliminary
data from the Ministry of Economy, Trade and Industry.


Looking ahead, manufacturers forecast 1.5 percent growth in
industrial production in March and a 1.3 percent gain in April,
the ministry said.
