

About 4200 dope tests will be undertaken at the 2008 Olympics in
Beijing, 20 percent more than in 2004, IOC medical director
Patrick Schamasch said on Friday.


There will be 694 extra tests in Beijing, compared with Athens,
and 441 of those will be made between the opening of the Olympic
village and the opening ceremony of which 258 tests will be on
urine and 183 on blood.
