

Turkey coach Fatih Terim announced a 26-man squad for Euro 2008
Group C qualifiers against Greece on March 24 in Athens and then
Norway four days later in Frankfurt, Germany:


Keepers: Hakan Arikan (Ankaraspor), Volkan Demirel (Fenerbahce),
Orkun Usak (Kayseri Erciyesspor)


Defenders: Emre Asik (Galatasaray), Servet Cetin (Sivasspor),
Huseyin Cimsir (Trabzonspor), Bekir Irtegun (Gaziantepspor), Nuri
Sahin (Borussia Dortmund/GER), Ibrahim Toraman (Besiktas),
Ibrahim Uzulmez (Besiktas), Gokhan Zan (Besiktas)


Midfielders: Hamit Altintop (Schalke 04/GER), Mehmet Aurelio
(Fenerbahce), Emre Belozoglu (Newcastle United/ENG), Ferdi Elmas
(Rizespor), Gokdeniz Karadeniz (Trabzonspor), Tugay Kerimoglu
(Blackburn Rovers/ENG), Sabri Sarioglu (Galatasaray), Mehmet
Topuz (Kayserispor), Arda Turan (Galatasaray)


Strikers: Tumer Metin (Fenerbahce), Tuncay Sanli (Fenerbahce),
Hakan Sukur (Galatasaray), Fatih Tekke (Zenit Petersburg/RUS),
Gokhan Unal (Kayserispor), Mehmet Yildiz (Sivasspor)
