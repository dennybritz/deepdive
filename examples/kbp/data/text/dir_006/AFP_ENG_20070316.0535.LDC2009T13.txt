

South Africa smashed a host of one-day records as they piled up
353-3 off their 40 overs in the rain-affected World Cup match
against the Netherlands at Warner Park on Friday:


- First man to hit six sixes in ODI over: Herschelle Gibbs


- Most team sixes in a ODI innings: 18


- Fastest World Cup 50: Mark Boucher, 21 balls
