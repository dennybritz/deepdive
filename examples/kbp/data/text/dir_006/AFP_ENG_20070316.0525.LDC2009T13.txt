

Greece coach Otto Rehhagel Friday named a 23-man squad for the
upcoming Euro 2008 qualifying matches against Turkey at home on
March 24 and Malta away four days later:


Squad


Goalkeepers: Antonis Nikopolidis (Olympiakos), Costas Halkias
(Aris Salonica)


Defenders: Giorgos Anatolakis and Vasilis Torosidis (Olympiakos),
Lukas Vyntra and Ioannis Goumas (Panathinaikos), Giourkas
Seitaridis (Atletico Madrid/ESP), Sotiris Kyrgiakos (Eintracht
Frankfurt/GER), Panayiotis Fyssas (Hearts/SCO) , Michalis Kapsis
(APOEL/CYP), Traianos Dellas (AEK Athens)


Midfielders: Costas Katsouranis (Benfica/POR), Alexandros Tziolis
(Panathinaikos), Georgios Karagounis (Benfica/POR), Angelos
Basinas (Mallorca/ESP), Stelios Giannakopoulos (Bolton/ENG),
Ioannis Amanatidis (Eintracht Frankfurt/GER)


Forwards: Dimitris Papadopoulos and Dimitris Salpingidis
(Panathinaikos), Angelos Charisteas (Feyenoord/NED), Georgios
Samaras (Manchester City/ENG), Fanis Gekas (VfL Bochum/GER),
Nikos Limberopoulos (AEK Athens)
