

Five people were killed and 51 injured Saturday when a passenger
plane made an emergency landing in Samara, 1,100 kilometers (660
miles) east of Moscow, officials were quoted as saying by Russian
news agencies.


"The plane made a harsh landing. As a result the plane's fuselage
broke apart. Five people were killed in the crash and 51 people
received various injuries," agency RIA Novosti quoted an
emergency situations ministry spokesperson as saying.
