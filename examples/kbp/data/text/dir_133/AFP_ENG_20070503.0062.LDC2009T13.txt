

Europe's main stock exchanges extended gains in opening deals on
Thursday after Wall Street's blue-chip index hit a fresh record
closing high overnight.


The London FTSE 100 index climbed 0.15 percent to reach 6,494.30
points, in Frankfurt the DAX 30 added 0.22 percent to 7,472.14
points and in Paris the CAC 40 won 0.38 percent to 6,013.11.
