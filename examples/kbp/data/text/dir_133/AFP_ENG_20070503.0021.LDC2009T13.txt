

Amare Stoudemire had 29 points and 17 rebounds as the Phoenix
Suns advanced to the second round of the National Basketball
Association playoffs with a 119-110 win over the Los Angeles
Lakers on Wednesday.


Shawn Marion had 26 points while Steve Nash added eight assists
for the Suns who eliminated the Lakers in five games.


The Suns advance to the next round of the Western Conference
playoffs where they will face the San Antonio Spurs who beat
Denver 93-78 to wrap up their series earlier Wednesday.
