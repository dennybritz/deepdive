

Olympic Council of Asia president Sheikh Ahmad Al-Fahad Al-Sabah
was Tuesday re- elected to a fifth term in office.


The former army officer and Kuwaiti oil minister, also an
International Olympic Committee member, has been president since
1991 and was the sole candidate in elections at the OCA congress
here.


The 44-year-old will remain in charge until 2011.


Secretary general Randhir Singh, from India, was also re-elected.


The OCA is the governing body of sports in Asia, including the
Asian Games.
