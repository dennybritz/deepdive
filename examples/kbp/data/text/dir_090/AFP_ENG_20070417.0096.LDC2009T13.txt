

Palestinian gunmen shot and wounded four Israelis in their car on
Tuesday near a Jewish settlement in the occupied West Bank, the
army said.


"Four Israelis were wounded this morning by Palestinian fire near
the Maale settlement," west of the Palestinian town of Ramallah,
an army spokesman said.


"One was wounded seriously and three others lightly. The four
were driving in a car when they were shot at by fire from another
vehicle," he said.


Earlier the army said one person was wounded in the shooting.
