

Czech coach Milan Macala has been named to take over at the helm
of the Bahrain national side, the Dnes daily newspaper reported
on Tuesday.


The 63-year-old former manager of the Czechoslovakia team before
the country split into the Czech Republic and Slovakia at the
start of 1993, has signed a contract taking him through until
2009.


Macala has years of experience in the Gulf having previously been
in charge of Kuwait, Saudi Arabia, the United Arab Emirates and
Oman. He was sacked from the Oman job in February.


"This will be a great challenge for me," he said. "I intend to do
all I can to bring success to Bahrain."
