

Australia beat England by seven wickets in their World Cup Super
Eights match at the Sir Vivian Richards Stadium here on Sunday.


Brief scores:


England 247 all out in 49.5 overs (K Pietersen 104, I Bell 77; N
Bracken 3-33, S Tait 3-41, G McGrath 3-62)


Australia 248-3 in 47.2 overs (R Ponting 86, M Clarke 55 no)


Australia won by 7 wickets
