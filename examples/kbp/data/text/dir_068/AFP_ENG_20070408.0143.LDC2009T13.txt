

South Korea beat Uzbekistan 5-0 in the second round of the Davis
Cup regional preliminaries Sunday, securing a spot in the play-
off for the tournament's top- level World Group.


Two Sunday wins gave South Korea the whitewash score after a
decisive 3-0 victory over the Uzbeks the previous day, and they
are now in the play-offs for a second consecutive year.


Jun Woong-Sun beat Ikramov Sarvar 7-5, 6-4, and An Jae-Sung
routed Uzakov Vaja 6-1, 6-2 on the final day of the Asia-Oceania
Zone Group One second-round tie.


On Saturday, An and Jun teamed up to defeat the Uzbek duo of
Dustov Farrukh and Istomin Dennis 6-1, 6-3, 6-1 in the doubles.


South Korean ace Lee Hyung-Taik beat Istomin 6-3, 6-2, 6-2 as Im
Kyu-Tae defeated Farrukh Dustov 6-0, 6-2, 4-6, 7-5 on Friday.


The winners of the second-round play-off advance to play for a
place in the World Group next year.


South Korea had beaten Kazakhstan in the first round.
