

Two near simultaneous car bombs at a police station in Baghdad on
Sunday killed nine people and wounded 28, security officials told
AFP.


Ten of those wounded in the coordinated attack on Baghdad's
southwestern Al- Bayaa police station were policemen, the
officials said, adding that the identity of the dead had to still
to be confirmed.


Insurgents have managed to set off bomb attacks in Baghdad
despite a massive security crackdown in the capital involving
nearly 80,000 US and Iraq troops.
