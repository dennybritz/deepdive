

Around 44.5 million people have registered to vote in next
month's presidential election in France, according to Interior
Ministry figures published Tuesday.


Exactly 43,973,024 people have registered in France and its
overseas territories, to which must be added some 535,000 who
live abroad and have registered at consulates.


As before previous presidential elections, there has been a
significant increase in registrations in the last 12 months, the
ministry said.
