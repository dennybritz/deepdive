

British retail sales leapt by 1.4 percent in February from the
figure in January, marking the biggest monthly gain since January
2005, official data showed on Thursday.


Retail sales were 4.9 percent higher than in February 2005, the
Office for National Statistics added. That was the biggest annual
increase since November 2004.


Analysts' consensus forecasts had been for a monthly gain of 0.5
percent an an annual increase of 3.7 percent.
