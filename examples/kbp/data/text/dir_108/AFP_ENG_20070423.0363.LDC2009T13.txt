

Former world 3,000m steeplechase champion Wilson Boit Kipketer of
Kenya is set to make his marathon debut in Dusseldorf on May 6,
organisers said Monday.


The 33-year-old, world champion in 1997 and Olympic steeplechase
silver medallist in 2000, is training with compatriot Wilfried
Kigen, winner of the Frankfurt marathon in 2005 and 2006.
