

England captain Michael Vaughan won the toss and elected to bat
in their World Cup Super Eights match against Ireland at the
Guyana National Stadium here on Friday.


England kept the same team which won the last first round match
against Kenya, while Ireland captain Trent Johnston was back
after missing his team's last match against the West Indies due
to a shoulder injury.


England: Michael Vaughan (capt), Ed Joyce, Ian Bell, Kevin
Pietersen, Paul Collingwood, Andrew Flintoff, Ravi Bopara, Paul
Nixon, Sajid Mahmood, James Anderson, Monty Panesar,


Ireland: William Porterfield, Jeremy Bray, Eoin Morgan, Niall
O'Brien, Andre Botha, Kevin O'Brien, Trent Johnston (capt), Kyle
McCallan, Andrew White, Dave Langford-Smith, Boyd Rankin,


Umpires: Simon Taufel (AUS) and Billy Doctrove (WIS)


Tv replays: Daryl Harper (AUS)


Reserve: Steve Bucknor (WIS)


Match referee: Ranjan Madugalle (SRI)


Pitch condition: Hard and slight green
